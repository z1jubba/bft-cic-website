Activities

What BFT is doing now.

Three focused activities form our starting point. Details can be updated as schedules and locations develop.

Environment

Neighbourhood litter picking

Volunteer clean-ups help improve streets, parks and shared spaces while encouraging local pride.

  • Open to local residents and volunteer groups
  • Locations announced before each session
  • Equipment arranged according to availability
Community support

Food distribution

We support the distribution of food to local people and families, working respectfully with community networks.

  • Distribution depends on available supply
  • Local partnership opportunities welcomed
  • Friendly and dignified support
Sport

Football at Sparkhill Park

Community football gives young people a positive way to stay active, socialise and build confidence.

  • Based at Sparkhill Park
  • Session details shared locally
  • Future expansion depends on funding and volunteers
